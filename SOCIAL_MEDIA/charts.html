Social Media Usage and Emotional Well-Being

Demographics

These charts will let you explore different demographic variables and couplings. To begin, you can take a look at the variables individually. Next , you can observe grouping information and can filter information yourself.

Dominant Emotion by Platform

This dashboard allows you to visualize how dominant emotions are distributed across platforms. To begin, you can select and or hover over a category on the right and see the number of users reporting that specific emotion category by platform.

Time Spent on Social Media

These charts provide a detailed analysis of the time spent on various social media platforms and its emotional impact. They illustrate the daily usage time by different genders, the types of interactions users engage in, and how this time is distributed across platforms like Facebook and Twitter.

To start, you can select an emotion from the chart, and watch as the other graphs update to show data specific to that emotion.

Interactions

This chart will provide the dominant emotions based on average daily numbers for the different metrics you choose.

The second chart shows which emotions have the highest and lowest average daily usage times (which has the most data) across all users.
